Why i like Virat kolhi

Virat Kohli is an inspiration to many, and it's easy to see why he stands out as a favorite. Kohli's aggressive yet calculated approach to batting has redefined modern cricket, earning him numerous records and accolades, including being one of the fastest players to reach 10,000 ODI runs.
